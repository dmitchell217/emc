Labourer with Landscaping and Renovation Expertise

  • Description
  • Demands
  • Conditions

We are seeking a dedicated labourer to join our dynamic team, ideally someone who possesses a truck.

The ideal candidate should have experience in maintaining external landscaping and providing non-technical labour for property renovations.

This role requires a hands-on approach, a strong work ethic, and a passion for transforming spaces.

  • Experience in external landscaping: Ability to cut, trim, and maintain outdoor spaces to enhance property aesthetics.
  • Renovation skills: Capable of handling various non-technical tasks, including but not limited to, demolition, cleanup, basic carpentry, and assisting specialized trades.
  • Owns a truck or has access to one: This is crucial for the transportation of materials, tools, and debris.
  • Physical fitness: The role often requires lifting, carrying, and other physically demanding tasks.
  • Reliable and punctual: Must adhere to schedules and ensure that tasks are completed in a timely manner.
  • Strong communication skills: Ability to communicate effectively with team members, property owners, and other stakeholders.
  • Willingness to learn: Open to acquiring new skills and techniques related to property renovation and landscaping.
  • Competitive compensation: Salary will be commensurate with experience and skills.
  • Work hours: This is a full-time position, with occasional weekend work depending on project requirements.
  • Training: While prior experience is desired, on-the-job training will be provided to ensure familiarity with company-specific practices.
  • Growth opportunities: As our company expands, there will be potential for growth and additional responsibilities for dedicated employees.
  • Supportive work environment: We pride ourselves on fostering a collaborative and supportive work atmosphere.
